Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards. It’s fun improving your memory and can help you in other spects of you life! In school, work, relationships or just not having to make that second trip to the store becuase you forgot something.
